I am passionate about building intelligent systems that solve meaningful problems. Driven by curiosity, I enjoy understanding how things work at their core and exploring the principles that make complex systems possible. What fascinates me most is the mathematical foundation behind technology, the ideas and abstractions that power the systems we use every day.

Beyond technical work, I have experience leading teams, organizing initiatives, and collaborating with people from diverse backgrounds. These experiences have strengthened my ability to communicate ideas clearly, take ownership of challenges, and work effectively in uncertain and fast-changing environments.

I believe that the best technology is not necessarily the most complex, but the one that solves the right problem in the simplest and most effective way. My goal is to build systems that are useful and are capable of creating meaningful impact.
